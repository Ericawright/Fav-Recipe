Easy Oven-Baked BBQ Pork Ribs

Serves 4

Prep time: 30 minutes

Cook time: 3 hours

Ingredients

BBQ Sauce

2 cups - Ketchup
1/2 cup - Brown Sugar
4 teaspoons - Salt
4 teaspoons - Mustard
1/2 cup - Vinegar
1/4 cup - Oil
(optional) 2-4 teaspoons - Hot Sauce

Other Ingredients

4 pounds - Pork Ribs

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
  • In a large bowl, combine ketchup, brown sugar, salt, mustard, vinegar, oil, and hot sauce.
  • Peel off tough membrane that covers the bony side of the ribs (if it exists).
  • Place ribs on a baking tray, bone side up.
  • Pour sauce over the ribs.
  • Cover baking tray with aluminum foil.
  • Bake until fork-tender, about 2 1/2 hours.
  • Cut into desired portions and serve.
